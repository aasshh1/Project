# IBM HR Employee Attrition Analysis

## Business Problem
Identify the key drivers of employee attrition to help HR teams
intervene before employees leave.

## Tools Used
Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn, Google Colab

## Key Findings
- Overtime is the #1 risk factor — employees on overtime leave at 3× the rate
- Sales Representatives have the highest attrition at 39.8%
- Employees in their first 2 years are most vulnerable at 28.9%
- High-risk profile (single, under 30, low income, overtime) shows 65.7% attrition
